## Top Competitors Analyzed

### UK Health/Fitness Sites

#### 1. Manual.co (Men's Health)
**Why it works:**
- **Hero:** Clear value proposition with 3 main treatments
- **Trust:** "UK Licensed medication" + "Free next-day delivery" + medical team photos
- **Social proof:** Trustpilot 4.5/5 with customer quotes
- **Design:** Clean white, navy blue accents, professional sans-serif fonts
- **CTA:** "View treatments" buttons everywhere
- **Structure:** Problem → Solution → Process → Team → Testimonials

**Colors:**
- Primary: Navy blue (#1a365d)
- Background: White/cream
- Accents: Light blue, medical green
- Typography: Inter or similar clean sans-serif

**What to copy:**
- Medical team section with credentials
- Treatment category cards (Hair/Testosterone/ED)
- Step-by-step process (How it works)
- Trust badges (UK licensed, free delivery)

---

#### 2. Numan.com (Men's Health)
**Why it works:**
- **Hero:** Bold headline "Own your health" with lifestyle imagery
- **Offers:** Discount banners (£94 off, 50% off) create urgency
- **Categories:** Weight loss, sexual health, TRT, blood tests, supplements
- **Trust:** Medical team credentials prominently displayed
- **Mobile:** Excellent responsive design

**Colors:**
- Primary: Deep teal/green (#0d7377)
- Background: White
- Accents: Coral/orange for CTAs
- Typography: Modern sans-serif

**What to copy:**
- Category grid layout
- Discount positioning
- "How it works" section with icons
- Treatment comparison tables

---

#### 3. Healthline.com (Health Content - US)
**Why it works:**
- **Navigation:** Sticky header with search
- **Articles:** Medical review badges ("Medically reviewed by Dr. X")
- **Structure:** TOC sidebar, fast facts, detailed content
- **Trust:** References/citations at bottom
- **Visual:** Custom illustrations, infographics
- **CTA:** Newsletter signup, condition-specific tools

**Colors:**
- Primary: Blue (#0070f3)
- Background: White
- Accents: Teal, purple
- Typography: System fonts (fast loading)

**What to copy:**
- Medical reviewer badges
- Table of contents navigation
- Reference sections with PubMed links
- Condition-specific tools/calculators
- Sticky social sharing

---

#### 4. Examine.com (Supplements)
**Why it works:**
- **Authority:** Research-based, citations everywhere
- **Tools:** Supplement comparison, interaction checker
- **Design:** Academic but accessible
- **Trust:** PhD researchers, systematic reviews

**What to copy:**

## Key Design Patterns Across Winners

### 1. Trust Signals
| Element | Manual | Numan | Healthline |
|---------|--------|-------|------------|
| Medical team | ✅ Photos + credentials | ✅ CMO photo | ✅ Review badges |
| Reviews | ✅ Trustpilot 4.5/5 | ✅ Testimonials | ✅ Medical review |
| Certifications | ✅ UK licensed | ✅ Prescription service | ✅ Editorial standards |
| Social proof | ✅ Customer quotes | ✅ Trust badges | ✅ Research citations |

### 2. Color Psychology
| Site | Primary | Accent | Feeling |
|------|---------|--------|---------|
| Manual | Navy blue | White/cream | Trustworthy, medical |
| Numan | Teal | Coral | Modern, approachable |
| Healthline | Blue | Purple | Clinical, authoritative |
| Second Nature | Green | White | Natural, healthy |

### 3. Layout Patterns

**Hero Section:**
- Headline: Clear benefit
- Subheadline: How it works
- CTA: Primary action button
- Trust: 2-3 trust badges below
- Image: Lifestyle or product

**Treatment/Category Cards:**
- Icon or image
- Title
- Brief description
- "Learn more" or "Get started" CTA

**How It Works:**
- Numbered steps (1, 2, 3)
- Icons for each step
- Brief explanation
- Visual flow (horizontal or vertical)

**Social Proof:**
- Star ratings
- Customer quotes
- Trust badges
- "Join X,000+ customers"

## Resources for "Stealing" Design

### Legal Ways to Learn from Competitors:
1. **Screenshot** — Save for reference
2. **Inspect Element** — See CSS structure
3. **Wappalyzer** — See what tech they use
4. **GTmetrix** — Check performance
5. **Copy structure** — Not code

### What NOT to Do:
- ❌ Copy CSS/HTML directly
- ❌ Use their exact images
- ❌ Clone their branding
- ❌ Copy written content

### What TO Do:
- ✅ Learn from layout patterns
- ✅ Adapt color psychology
- ✅ Use similar components
- ✅ Create your own content
