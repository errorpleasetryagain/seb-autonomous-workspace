## Link Strategy Rules

1. **Natural placement** — Links where ingredients are mentioned
2. **Contextual value** — Explain WHY this product matters
3. **Price transparency** — Show approximate cost
4. **Alternatives noted** — "Or from supermarket" for fresh items
5. **Equipment as investment** — "Lasts a lifetime" for quality tools

## Categories for Bulk Linking

### Always Link
- Specialty ingredients (mirin, miso, ghee)
- Equipment over £10
- Bulk pantry staples (rice, pasta cases)
- Subscription services (Amazon Fresh)

### Sometimes Link
- Standard spices (when in sets)
- Canned goods (when bulk)
- Protein (when frozen bulk)

### Never Link
- Fresh produce (supermarket only)
- Single eggs
- Generic "salt and pepper"
