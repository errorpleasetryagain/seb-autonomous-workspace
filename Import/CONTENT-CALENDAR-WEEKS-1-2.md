# Content Calendar — Weeks 1–2

Publishing cadence: 2 articles per site per week = 14 articles per site over the first 2 weeks.
Since we have 7 sites, that's 14 total articles per week across the portfolio (2 per site).

**Strategy:** Lead with cornerstone/pillar content first — these are the articles that anchor each site's SEO, build topical authority, and contain the highest-value affiliate links. Long-tail blog posts come in Phase 2.

## Publishing Notes

- All articles should be 2,000–3,000 words minimum for cornerstone content
- Include at least one comparison table with affiliate links per article
- Every article needs: meta title, meta description, 3–5 internal links, 2–3 external authority links
- Images: use Cloudinary for hosting, optimise for WebP
- Each article should have a clear CTA and email capture prompt
- UK English throughout, Huberman-style but accessible tone
