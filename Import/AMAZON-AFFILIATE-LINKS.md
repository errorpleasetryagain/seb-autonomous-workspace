## NOTES

- **90-day clock:** Already ticking from account creation. Need one qualifying sale before day 90 or account closes. Get these links live ASAP.
- **Use SiteStripe:** When logged into amazon.co.uk with your Associates account, a bar appears at the top. Click "Text" on any product page to get a clean tagged link instantly — faster than building URLs manually.
- **Search links vs ASIN links:** Search links (e.g. `/s?k=magnesium+glycinate`) still earn commission and are safer long-term (don't break if a product goes out of stock). ASIN links convert slightly better.
