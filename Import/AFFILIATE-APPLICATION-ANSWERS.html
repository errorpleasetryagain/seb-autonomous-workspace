"Monthly Traffic" / "Audience Size"

Be honest here. The sites are new so traffic is still growing. Most programmes care more about content quality than traffic numbers at this stage.

If they ask for exact numbers

Our sites are newly launched with a growing library of 20-150+ published articles each. We are in the early growth phase focused on organic SEO traffic. Current monthly visitors are in the low hundreds but growing as content indexes. We also have a subscriber email list and social media presence across YouTube, Instagram, X, and LinkedIn.

If they give you a dropdown and your traffic is below the minimum

Pick the lowest option available. If the minimum is "1,000+" and you're below that, you can still apply. Many programmes approve based on content quality rather than current traffic. If rejected, reapply in 2-3 months when traffic has grown.
