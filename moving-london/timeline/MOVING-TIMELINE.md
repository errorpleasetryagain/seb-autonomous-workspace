## Critical Deadlines

| Deadline | Task | Consequence if Missed |
|----------|------|----------------------|
| End of April | Start viewings | Risk of not finding place by July |
| End of May | Secure tenancy | July move becomes tight |
| 2 weeks before move | Book removals | Limited availability, higher prices |
| 1 week before move | Utilities setup | No gas/electric on arrival |
| Move day | Meter readings | Could be charged for previous tenant's usage |
